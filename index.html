Los Angeles Hindu Society is dedicated to spreading the love and message of Hinduism through worship, fellowship and service. We strive to create a welcoming and inclusive community where everyone is valued and supported in their faith journey.

Services

Following services are offered at our temple. Please call the temple office or the priest to schedule events in advance. To schedule services at your home, please call him well ahead to book your appointments

At the temple

All types of abhishekams, homam, special poojas and abishekams, birthdays, rememberance days, thivasam / aandu (annual) thivasam, etc.

At your home

Vasthu Puja with or without Homam, Namaharnam, Punyagam, Ayush Homam, Vidyarambam, Wedding (any place), Graha Pravesam, All types of house Prayers, Graha Thosha, Thrushdy Pariharam, Shanthi Pariharam, Srartham, Thithi and Thivasam, etc.
